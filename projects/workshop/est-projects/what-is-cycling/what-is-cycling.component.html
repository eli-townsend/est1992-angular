What Is Cycling is a multi episode video series that features individual cyclist around the community and the different types of riding that makes them unique.

What Is Cycling is not just a video series but a community project that I started to hopefully make cycling all inclusive so that everyone feels welcome and not intimidated. I heard a saying that goes like this, "if you are in a community, be apart of the community." Cycling is my community and this is how I want to help out with the community. It is simple, when I bike, I am going around and meeting people along the way and asking them different sets of questions:

  1. Name? Where are you located? How long you have been riding?
  2. What is your favorite type of riding?
  3. What is cycling to you?
  4. What advice would you give to someone getting started?
  5. Specialty question depending on rider
